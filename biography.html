Biography

Short Bio

Chen Chen received the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering at the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Lehigh University, Bethlehem, PA in 2013, under the supervision of Prof. Shalinee Kishore. Before that, he received the Master and Bachelor degrees from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China in 2009 and 2006, respectively. During 2013 to 2015, he was a Postdoctoral Appointee at Argonne National Laboratory in Lemont, Illinois. During 2016 to 2019, he was an Energy Systems Scientist in the Center for Energy, Environmental, and Economic Systems Analysis at Argonne National Laboratory. He also had a joint appointment at The University of Chicago. Since 2019, he joined School of Electrical Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University as a Professor.

Dr. Chen is serving as a chair for Special Interest Group of Cyber Security, Privacy, and Resilience for the Smart Grid, in IEEE Communication Society Smart Grid Communications Committee. He has been serving as an editor of IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid since 2015. He is the recipient of the IEEE Power and Energy Society Chicago Chapter Outstanding Engineer Award in 2018.
