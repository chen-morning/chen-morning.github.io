Research Interests

My research interests generally include applying optimization, communications, and signal processsing techniques for smart grids. Currently, I am interested in the following topics:

  • Electric power system resilience

  • Cyber-physical system modeling and analysis

  • Infrastructure interdependency modeling and analysis
